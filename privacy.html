What information does the Application LoadFit obtain and how is it used?

Apple Health Data: The health data we access from Apple Health is used solely for the purpose of analyzing your training load.

The application collects some usage data that are essential for us to deliver our services, to understand your needs and to improve our services. The analytics services automatically collect certain information that does not personally identify our end users who access or use our mobile applications. This information includes but is not limited to device state information, unique device identifiers, installation UUID, device hardware and OS information, information relating to how application functions or anonymized IP addresses.

Data Storage and Security

Apple Health Data: We do not store any Apple Health data remotely. All health data remains on your device and is processed locally.

Access and Control: You have the right to access and control your personal information. You can manage the health data shared with LoadFit via the Apple Health app settings on your device.
